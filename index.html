What Are We?

We’re more than just a cycling team—we’re a crew of passionate riders brought together by fun, friendship, and the thrill of the road. Whether we’re cruising local routes or jumping into semi-competitive races hosted by fellow teams, we ride for the joy of it. No strict rules, no pressure—just good vibes, spinning wheels, and shared adventures. From weekend sprints to long hauls with laughs and snacks, we ride as one. Call us a team, a family, or a rolling circus—we're here for the ride and the stories that come with it.

Our Mission

To foster a vibrant community of riders driven by camaraderie, personal growth, and the pure joy of cycling—supporting each other through every climb, sprint, and finish line.

Our Vision

To become a known and respected cycling group admired not just for our pedal power, but for our spirit, inclusivity, and commitment to the journey—on and off the road.

About Us

Who We Are

Maharlika Cycling Team is a fun-loving crew of riders based in Legazpi City. We’re not bound by rules or rigid training schedules—we ride for the thrill, the laughs, and the shared stories on the road. Our team is made up of riders from all walks of life, united by two wheels and a love for the outdoors.

Our Ride Culture

We value friendship, inclusivity, and the joy of riding more than competition. You’ll find us cruising through city streets, climbing rural trails, or rolling deep into weekend “semi-legal” races hosted by other teams. Whether you're a newbie or a seasoned rider, you're welcome here.

What Makes Us Different

We’re not just a cycling group—we’re a rolling family. We don’t just train—we explore, connect, and share the road. From planning spontaneous rides to late-night food stops and after-ride hangouts, Maharlika is a vibe you ride with.
